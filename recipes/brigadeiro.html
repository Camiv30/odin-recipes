Brigadeiro

Brigadeiro is a great Brazilian dessert similar to bonbons that's extremely scrumptious and delicious. This sweet is loved in Brazil. Try it! You can also make it in the microwave; just remember to stir every minute.

Prep time: 15 mins

Servings: 20 servings

Ingredients

  • 1 (14 ounce) can of sweetened condensed milk
  • 3 tablespoons of unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 tablespoon of butter

Directions

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Combine the condensed milk, cocoa powder and butter in a medium saucepan over medium heat. Cook and stir until thickened and it's not sticking at the bottom of the pan, about 10 minutes. Remove from heat and let it rest until mixture is cool enough to handle
  3. Use your hands to shape into small balls and place on a serving plate. Can be eaten at once or chilled until serving.
